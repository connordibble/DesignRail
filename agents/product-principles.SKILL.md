# Product Principles Skill

## Use when

Work affects product behavior, workflow priority, UX decisions, or scope tradeoffs.

## Goal

Keep DesignRail focused on a trustworthy human-review product, not a script demo.

## Rules

- The review UI is the product surface.
- Optimize for developer confidence, traceability, and fast review decisions.
- AI recommendations must be inspectable, editable, and reversible.
- Prefer clear status, rationale, and next action over automation theater.
- Preserve a useful mock-mode experience at all times.
